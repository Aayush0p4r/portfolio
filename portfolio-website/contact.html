Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I specialize in full-stack web applications, e-commerce platforms, SaaS products, and custom business solutions. I work with startups, established companies, and agencies on projects ranging from MVPs to enterprise-scale applications.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications can take 8-16 weeks or more. I always provide detailed project estimates with milestones and deliverables.

Do you work with existing teams?

Absolutely! I frequently collaborate with in-house development teams, design agencies, and other contractors. I'm experienced with various workflows, code review processes, and project management tools.

What's included in your development process?

My process includes requirements analysis, technical planning, development, testing, deployment, and post-launch support. I prioritize clear communication, regular updates, and collaborative decision-making throughout the project.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes! I offer various maintenance and support packages including bug fixes, feature updates, performance monitoring, and technical consultation. Many clients prefer ongoing retainer relationships for continuous improvements.
